Using the Application

This GIS Citizen Comment Collector web application allows citizens to submit their comments, requests and ideas for different Civiltech projects. It is a single application that can be used on a desktop browser, mobile phone, and tablet device. In short, the comment collector application allows citizens to submit comments to a specific project study area. Pan and zoom around, click/tap directly on the map to pin your comment to the map. Then, provide details about your comment and click/tap 'Submit' to finalize your request. Providing your name, phone and/or email is OPTIONAL. This information will be seen by Civiltech Engineers and will NOT be available to other users.

Place a Comment on The Map

You can interact directly with the map by panning or zooming around. When you have found your comment location, simply click/tap to place your comment point. If you accidentally place a comment marker in the wrong location, you can "cancel" the placement and replace the marker again. Once comments are submitted, a user cannot go back to edit or change their response.

Map Based Navigation (On a Desktop PC)

There are several ways to interact with the map:

1. Use the basemap switcher to select the basemap you'd like to interact with
2. Hold down the shift key and drag a box on the map to zoom in
3. Hold Down Ctrl+Shift to zoom out
4. Roll your mouse roller to zoom in/out
5. Click the zoom in/out button

Map Based Navigation (On a Touch Screen Device)

There are two ways to interact with the map:

1. Pinch to zoom in/out
2. Tap the zoom in/out button

Adding Your Comment to the Map

Click directly on the map to pin your comment to a specific location. Then, provide details about the request and click 'Submit' to initiate your comment.

The Citizen Service Request application is supported on the following browsers: Microsoft IE 7/8/9, Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome and Apple Safari.
